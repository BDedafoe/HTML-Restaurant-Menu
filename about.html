Origin Story

Chef and owner Joe Chung wanted to bring his favorite street foods from Thailand to the US. After working in multiple Michelin Star restaurants, Joe opened his first downtown Chicago location to bring his favorite foods from his homeland to the masses. Taking his added culinary experiences, this Thai inspired menu will leave you wanting more.
